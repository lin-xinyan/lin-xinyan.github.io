2022年7月文书段落

2022年7月文书段落

Tell us about who you are. How would your family, friends, and/or members of your community describe you? If possible, please include something about yourself that you are most proud of and why.

I am a 17-year-old student at Nanjing Jinling High School and I enjoy studying very much. I get on well with my classmates and teachers. At home, I am the only child and I try to help my parents do all kinds of housework because they both work full-time.

Generally, my family and friends treat me very well and they all think I am a friendly person. An advantage of this is that if others treat me well, I will do my best to return the favour. For example, if someone help me when I have some difficulties, I will give all my effort to help them in the future. An additional observation from them is that I always demonstrate strong perseverance in my studies. Once I start focusing on writing an essay, I am eager to complete it to the highest of standards so I obtain a high mark. Even if there are many hard times and obstacles, I will not give up easily. For example, when I find some academic terms which I cannot understand, I am determined to understand their meaning by reading text books and searching the Internet. Moreover, people around me also say that I am willing to think creatively and surprise others by what I have done. On one occasion, our class needed to make a poster to introduce ourselves so I asked every student to print their hand-prints on a large piece of paper, in the shape of circle to show our unity, which got high praise from both students and teachers.

Getting into my dream high school has made me feel proud, because I gave all my effort to pass the tests with the best scores possible. Before the examination, I reviewed all my books and did the right amount of revision. Although this school is not the best one in Nanjing, I am still satisfied about becoming a student of it.

Overall, I hope to continuously improve myself and strive for the maximum improvement for myself.
