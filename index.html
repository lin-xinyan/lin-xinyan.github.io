2022年8月文书段落

Briefly describe the culture of your school community and your involvement within it. What impact has the school culture had on you? How would you enhance or change it?

As Jinling High School was built in 1888, it has a strong cultural heritage. The school motto of “honesty, diligence and benevolence” has inspired my high school to cultivate many excellent students with all-round development.

Since I started the high school, I have worked hard to reach the standard of the school motto. First of all, I always treat people honestly. I have never lied to my classmates and my teachers, which helps me build a good reputation. Secondly, since my friends have different skills and attributes, I always retain a modest attitude which enables me to learn from them. As a result, I have developed my own strategies to solve problems. Moreover, my classmates and I often attend social activities to help people in need. For example, during the winter of 2021, we held a dance show to raise money for children in remote areas to receive a better education. This demonstrates our benevolence.

I think the culture of my high school has greatly improved my skills in collaborating and helping others. In high school, I have gradually changed from a person who is not good at expressing myself to an outgoing person. In junior high, my classmates would always try to cover up their own feelings to meet others’ opinions, which helped them avoid problems and confrontations. But when I started in high school, the fellow students around me were very magnanimous and they did not mind offering different opinions with confidence. Given these positive role models, I was encouraged to be more open. This approach and attitude is very liberating and powerful.

However, I also feel that the campus culture can be improved. Due to the unavoidable heavy academic burden of high school students, the development of activities of some culture groups is relatively slow. After some discussions with members of the students’ union, we decided to add several clubs related to the intangible cultural heritage to teach students more about Chinese traditional crafts. It is hoped this will lead to further interest in areas of Chinese culture but also maintain high academic achievement which the school excels in

2022年7月文书段落

Tell us about who you are. How would your family, friends, and/or members of your community describe you? If possible, please include something about yourself that you are most proud of and why.

I am a 17-year-old student at Nanjing Jinling High School and I enjoy studying very much. I get on well with my classmates and teachers. At home, I am the only child and I try to help my parents do all kinds of housework because they both work full-time.

Generally, my family and friends treat me very well and they all think I am a friendly person. An advantage of this is that if others treat me well, I will do my best to return the favour. For example, if someone help me when I have some difficulties, I will give all my effort to help them in the future. An additional observation from them is that I always demonstrate strong perseverance in my studies. Once I start focusing on writing an essay, I am eager to complete it to the highest of standards so I obtain a high mark. Even if there are many hard times and obstacles, I will not give up easily. For example, when I find some academic terms which I cannot understand, I am determined to understand their meaning by reading text books and searching the Internet. Moreover, people around me also say that I am willing to think creatively and surprise others by what I have done. On one occasion, our class needed to make a poster to introduce ourselves so I asked every student to print their hand-prints on a large piece of paper, in the shape of circle to show our unity, which got high praise from both students and teachers.

Getting into my dream high school has made me feel proud, because I gave all my effort to pass the tests with the best scores possible. Before the examination, I reviewed all my books and did the right amount of revision. Although this school is not the best one in Nanjing, I am still satisfied about becoming a student of it.

Overall, I hope to continuously improve myself and strive for the maximum improvement for myself.

Markdown语法介绍

Title：Markdown语法介绍

1.大纲级别介绍

一级大纲 ：一个#加上一个空格

二级大纲 ：两个#加上一个空格

三级大纲 ：三个#加上一个空格

四级大纲 ：四个#加上一个空格

五级大纲 ：五个#加上一个空格
六级大纲 ：六个#加上一个空格

2.分割线

至少三个减号

3.字体

斜体文本 ：两个星号之间的文章段落为斜体
粗体文本 ：两个星号和两个星号之间的文章段落为粗体
粗斜体文本 ：三个星号和三个星号之间的文章段落为粗斜体

4.删除符号

今天是晴天
两个波浪线和两个波浪线之间（波浪线在键盘上位于数字1左边）

5.下划线

今天是晴天
在要下划线的文字左边加上尖括号，里头加上小写字母u；在文字右边也加上尖括号，里面加上/u

6.列表

无序列表

  • 第一项
  • 第二项
  • 第三项
    在第一项前面加上一个“-”和一个空格，转行时第二项自动出现。结束列表，不按回车键进入下一行

有序列表

  1. 第一项
  2. 第二项
  3. 第三项
    在第一项前面加上1.和一个空格，转行时第二项自动出现。结束列表，不按回车键进入下一行

列表的嵌套

  1. 第一大项：
    • 第一项
    • 第二项
    • 第三项
  2. 第二大项：
    • 第一项
    • 第二项
    • 第三项

7.引用

引用
一个大于号加上一个空格

8.代码

print f( )
两个反引号之间加上函数（反引号在键盘上位于数字1左边）

9.代码段

三个反引号+代码段落+三个反引号，两组反引号在两行

10.markdown超链接

[百度] (https://www.baidu.com)
中括号（英文）里加上网站名，加上小括号（英文）里加上网站链接

11.markdown表格

: —– :—-: —- :
向左对齐 居中 向右对齐

12.上传图片

  1. 上传图片到[路过图床] (https://imgchr.com/)
  2. 复制Markdown code
